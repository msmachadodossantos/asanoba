# About the Color Palette

The palette strikes a balance between **warm and cool tones**, conveying a visual identity that blends **warmth, clarity, and vitality**.

### `#F27565` – Soft Coral  
An emotional and modern color. Evokes a sense of **warmth, empathy, and gentle energy**. Ideal for creating a welcoming and contemporary atmosphere.

### `#FFD250` – Light Yellow  
A vibrant and optimistic hue. Represents **light, creativity, and positivity**. Perfect for elements meant to attract attention in a friendly and soft way.

### `#4A8ACF` – Medium Blue  
Conveys **stability, trust, and calmness**. Serves as a strong, reliable base with a fresh and professional tone.

### `#5FAE81` – Medium Green  
Associated with **growth, balance, and well-being**. Complements the palette with a sense of nature and renewal.

### `#F2F6FA` – Light Neutral  
A soft, neutral tone that offers **clarity and visual breathing room**. Great for backgrounds or balancing more vibrant elements.

## General Considerations

This color composition offers **versatility and harmony**, with strong emotional and visual appeal. It is well-suited for applications seeking to express **positivity, balance, and trust**, while maintaining a light and welcoming aesthetic.
